# Visual Pack Guide (Non-Diagram Assets)

This folder provides polished SVG visuals for your presentation and thesis.

## Files
- `cover_hero.svg`: Title-slide hero background.
- `icon_privacy_governance.svg`: Privacy and governance visual.
- `icon_hybrid_retrieval.svg`: Hybrid retrieval concept icon.
- `icon_evaluation.svg`: Evaluation and metrics icon.
- `screenshot_frame_16_9.svg`: Clean frame for UI screenshots.
- `section_divider_wave.svg`: Decorative section divider.

## Slide Suggestions
- Slide 1: `cover_hero.svg`
- Slide 2 or 12: `icon_privacy_governance.svg`
- Slide 9: `icon_hybrid_retrieval.svg`
- Slide 11 or 13: `icon_evaluation.svg`
- Slide 11: `screenshot_frame_16_9.svg`
- Optional section transitions: `section_divider_wave.svg`

## Report Suggestions
- Chapter opening pages: `section_divider_wave.svg` (small footer style)
- Chapter 1 and 5: `icon_privacy_governance.svg`
- Chapter 4: `icon_evaluation.svg`
- Appendix with UI evidence: place screenshots inside `screenshot_frame_16_9.svg`

## Editing and Export
- Open SVG in PowerPoint or Figma and recolor quickly.
- You can keep vectors as SVG in slides for crisp quality.
- Export to PNG at 2x for Word report embedding if needed.
